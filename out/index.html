Why TurtleShaders?

Carefully crafted features to make Minecraft look stunning while keeping performance smooth.

High Performance

Optimized for all GPUs. Smooth 60+ FPS on mid-range hardware with intelligent rendering pipelines.

Beautiful Lighting

Physically based lighting with realistic shadows, ambient occlusion, and global illumination.

Dynamic Sky

Cinematic skyboxes with volumetric clouds, stars, sunsets, and weather effects that change over time.

Water Reflections

Stunning reflective water with wave simulation, caustics, and realistic transparency.

Volumetric Clouds

3D cloud rendering with soft edges, god rays, and dynamic lighting for immersive skies.

Open Source

Fully open source under a permissive license. Free forever. Contribute on GitHub.

Community Driven

Built with love by the community. Feature requests, bug reports, and contributions are always welcome.

Easy Installation

Drag and drop installation. Compatible with all major Minecraft shader loaders including Iris and OptiFine.

About TurtleShaders

TurtleShaders is an open-source Minecraft Java shader pack focused on delivering beautiful visuals while remaining lightweight and customizable. Developed by GamesofDev, it is designed for players who want vibrant lighting, cinematic skies, improved water, and smooth performance. The project welcomes community contributions and aims to make high-quality shaders accessible to everyone.
